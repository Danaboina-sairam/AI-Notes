✅ Linear Algebra – The Core

Most ML algorithms are matrix math under the hood.
• Vectors & Matrices: addition, multiplication, transpose
• Dot Product & Norms: cosine similarity, distance metrics
• Eigenvalues / SVD: PCA, dimensionality reduction

- Interview question: "Explain how embeddings or PCA work"

⸻

✅ Probability & Statistics – For Uncertainty

• Random Variables & Distributions (Gaussian, Bernoulli, etc.)
• Expectation & Variance
• Bayes’ Theorem: conditional probabilities, Naïve Bayes (super important)
• Hypothesis Testing & p-values

- Interview angle: "How would you evaluate a classifier with imbalanced data?"

📌 Statistics & Probability

1. What’s the difference between standard deviation and standard error, and when do we use each?
2. Why is the Central Limit Theorem important in hypothesis testing?
3. How do confidence intervals help interpret model predictions?
4. Can a high correlation still mean there's no causation? Give a real-world example.
5. What’s the intuition behind p-values, and why are they often misinterpreted?
6. In what situations does the mean give misleading information compared to the median?
7. What is heteroscedasticity, and why is it a problem in regression analysis?
